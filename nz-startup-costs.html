Registration & Structure

Item Notes Cost (NZD)
Sole Trader Registration Once-off No Companies Office fee required; just a free IRD number $0
IRD Number Once-off Required for all business structures; free to obtain Free
Company Incorporation Once-off Online registration via Companies Office ~$105 + GST
Reserve Company Name Once-off Optional but recommended before incorporating $10 + GST
Partnership Agreement Once-off Template (~$20) or lawyer-drafted (~$400) $20–$400
Annual Return (Company) Annual Filed each year with Companies Office ~$50 + GST

Tax & Compliance

Item Notes Cost (NZD)
GST Registration Once-off Mandatory if turnover exceeds $60,000/yr; free to register Free
Income Tax — Company Annual Flat rate on profit 28% of profit
Income Tax — Sole Trader Annual Paid at personal income rates 10.5%–39%
ACC Levies Annual Earner's levy ($1.75/$100), Working Safer ($0.08/$100) + Work levy by industry $500–$3,000+
Accounting / Tax Filing Annual Varies by complexity; hire an accountant or DIY $1,000–$5,000
Accounting Software Annual e.g. Xero or MYOB; ~$35–$113/month $420–$1,356

Legal & Intellectual Property

Item Notes Cost (NZD)
Legal Setup Fees Once-off Shareholder agreements, contracts; fixed-fee services $500–$1,500
Trademark Registration Once-off Per class of goods/services via IPONZ $50–$100/class
Business Licences / Permits Varies Depends on industry (food, financial services, trades, etc.) $0–$5,000+

Operations

Item Notes Cost (NZD)
Business Bank Account Once-off Most NZ banks offer free or low-cost business accounts $0–$200
Website & Domain Annual .nz domain ~$25/yr; basic hosting ~$100–$500/yr $100–$600
Business Insurance Annual Public liability, professional indemnity (varies widely) $500–$3,000+
Office / Workspace Annual Home office = minimal cost; leased space requires deposit + fit-out $0–$20,000+

Year 1 Totals (Typical Range)

Business Type Description Est. Year 1 Cost

💡 Tip: Add 20–30% buffer For unexpected costs, price rises, and compliance surprises Always recommended
Key thresholds to know: GST registration is mandatory once turnover hits $60,000/year. ACC levies are compulsory for all working New Zealanders. Company tax is a flat 28%; sole traders pay personal income rates. All fees above are in NZD and exclude GST unless stated. Figures are indicative for 2025–26 and subject to change.
