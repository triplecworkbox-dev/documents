🇳🇿NZ Limited Company — Startup Costs

Year 1 costs for registering and running a New Zealand limited liability company (NZD, excl. GST unless noted)

Registration & Structure

ItemNotesCost (NZD)
Reserve Company Name Once-off Via Companies Office; holds name for 20 working days $10 + GST
Company Incorporation Once-off Online via Companies Office; requires reserved name + IRD number ~$105 + GST
IRD Number (Company) Once-off Apply online after incorporation; required before banking or trading Free
Annual Return Annual Filed each year with Companies Office to keep company active ~$50 + GST

Tax & Compliance

ItemNotesCost (NZD)
GST Registration Once-off Mandatory if turnover exceeds $60,000/yr; free to register via IRD Free
Company Income Tax Annual Flat 28% on net profit; provisional tax if liability > $5,000 28% of profit
ACC Levies Annual Earner's ($1.75/$100) + Working Safer ($0.08/$100) + Work levy by industry $500–$3,000+
Accounting / Tax Filing Annual Annual accounts + tax return; companies require more than sole traders $1,500–$5,000
Accounting Software Annual Xero or MYOB recommended; ~$35–$113/month $420–$1,356

Legal & Intellectual Property

ItemNotesCost (NZD)
Shareholder Agreement Once-off Strongly recommended for multiple shareholders; lawyer-drafted or template $500–$2,000
Company Constitution Once-off Optional — Companies Act default rules apply if not provided $0–$800
Trademark Registration Once-off Per class via IPONZ; protects brand name & logo in NZ $50–$100/class
Business Licences / Permits Varies Industry-dependent (food, financial services, trades, etc.) $0–$5,000+

Operations

ItemNotesCost (NZD)
SIM / Mobile Number Once-off Needed for 2FA on bank & registrations; personal SIM is fine $5–$30
Business Bank Account Once-off Requires company number + IRD; most NZ banks free or low-cost $0–$200
Domain Registration Annual .nz domain ~$25/yr; personal SIM usable for verification $20–$50
Website Hosting Annual Shared to managed hosting; separate from domain registration $100–$600
Business Insurance Annual Public liability + professional indemnity; varies by industry & revenue $500–$3,000+
Office / Workspace Annual Home office ≈ $0 extra; lease requires deposit + possible fit-out $0–$20,000+

Year 1 Estimated Total — Limited Company

ScenarioIncludesEst. Year 1 Cost
Lean (home-based, no staff) Incorporation, accounting, ACC, software, domain $3,500–$6,000
Mid-size (leased office, 1–3 staff) Above + rent, insurance, payroll compliance $15,000–$40,000+
💡 Add a 20–30% buffer Unexpected compliance, delays, professional fees Always recommended
Key points: A company needs an IRD number before opening a bank account. GST registration is mandatory at $60,000 turnover. Company tax is a flat 28% on profit. Provisional tax kicks in if your tax liability exceeds $5,000. All figures are indicative for 2025–26 and exclude GST unless stated.
